Fast-Finish Quilted Mug Rug

The Perfect Handmade Holiday Gift

The holiday season is here, and the best gifts are always the ones you make yourself! This Fast-Finish Quilted Mug Rug is a quick, satisfying project you can complete in an afternoon. It's perfect for using up scraps and making thoughtful, personalized gifts for teachers, neighbors, or colleagues. Follow this step-by-step guide to sew a whole set this weekend!

üßµ Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Prep and Baste the Quilt Sandwich

Ensure all layers are pressed smooth before you begin.

  1. Lay Out: Place your backing fabric (Fabric 2) face down on your work surface.
  2. Add Batting: Center the batting piece directly on top of the backing fabric.
  3. Add Top: Center the mug rug top (Fabric 1) face up on top of the batting.
  4. Baste: Secure all three layers together using safety pins (spaced every few inches) or temporary basting spray. This prevents shifting during quilting.
[Image: Quilt layers properly arranged]
2
Quilt the Layers

The quilting adds necessary texture and structure, binding the three layers permanently.

  1. Choose a Design: For a simple and fast finish, we recommend straight-line quilting using your walking foot.
  2. Stitching: Start in the center and quilt straight lines every 1/2 inch, moving outward to the edges. You can quilt vertically, horizontally, or diagonally‚Äîor get fancy and do a simple grid!
[Image: Quilting lines on fabric]
3
Square Up and Trim

This step ensures clean, professional edges for attaching the binding.

  1. Find Your True Edges: Place your ruler on the quilted piece and align it with your quilting lines. Trim one edge to establish a straight line.
  2. Square Corners: Rotate the piece and use the newly trimmed edge to ensure the next cut is perfectly square (90 degrees). Trim all four sides.
  3. Final Size: Your final mug rug should measure exactly 8.5 inches x 5.5 inches.
[Image: Trimming the edges with a rotary cutter]
4
Attach the Binding

The binding finishes the raw edges and provides a clean frame for your work.

  1. Prepare Binding: Take your 2.5-inch strip of binding fabric and press it in half lengthwise.
  2. Attach to Front: Starting along the center of one edge, align the raw edge of the binding strip with the raw edge of the mug rug top. Use a 1/4-inch seam allowance to sew the binding to the front of the mug rug, mitering the corners as you reach each turn.
  3. Finish to Back: Fold the binding over the raw edge to the back of the mug rug. Secure it by hand stitching (for a classic look) or by machine stitching (for a quick finish).
[Image: Binding attached to mug rug]
